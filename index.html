#FOSSilized

Wondering about what exactly GLUG is ??

GLUG stands for GNU/Linux User's Group. GLUG RNSIT is a Student Group @RNSIT monitored by FSMK (Free Software Movement Karnataka) spreading the idea of Free Software Philosophy. "Free software" means software that respects users' freedom and community. Roughly, it means that the users have the freedom to run, copy, distribute, study, change and improve the software. Thus, "free software" is a matter of liberty, not price. To understand the concept, you should think of "free" as in "free speech," not as in "free beer" or "free biriyani".

Why join GLUG @ RNSIT?

Because being a member of GLUG @ RNSIT isn't just local, You will be part of a growing mass movement for free software. You will be part of something not just at a local level, but at a global level giving your acceptance for a global movement and you will become an activist driving this movement forward. Also you will also learn some cool stuff that can be done using only FOSS(Google right away) tools. Hands on Experience, Exposure to Linux, Group learning sessions, Help for implementing your project ideas are some of the other perks of being part of this group.
